Venue

Registration

To register for ICTIR, go to the SIGIR 2026 page and select "Registration" [coming real soon now].

If you plan to attend SIGIR as well as ICTIR, register as a "Three-Day" or "Five-Day" attendee, and ICTIR will be available as an optional extra as part of those registration sequences.

If you plan to attend ICTIR only, select the "One-Day" SIGIR registration option, and ICTIR will be one of the "days" that can be picked, together with zero or more of the days on which SIGIR activities will take place.

ICTIR registration may be completed at the same time as SIGIR registration is undertaken, or can be added at any later time. The ICTIR registration fees are constant, and there are no early discounts or late penalties.

Accommodation

The same SIGIR registration page allows accommodation options to be compared and booked.

Accommodation requests must be received before Wednesday 3 June, 11:59pm Melbourne time.

Please read the Registration and Accommodation Terms and Conditions on the SIGIR web site carefully.

Directions

ICTIR will be held at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre.

Address: 1 Convention Centre Place, South Wharf 3006
